Screenshot Redactor

Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device.

Screenshot Redactor was built with one principle: nothing about your images should ever leave your phone. All processing — face detection, text recognition, barcode scanning — runs entirely on-device using Android's on-device ML APIs.

🔒 No uploads 👤 No account 📊 No analytics 📱 100% on-device

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your images or personal data. Ever.

The app does not require an account, login, or any personal information to function. Images you select are processed locally in memory and are never written to any external server or third-party service.

Permissions Used

INTERNET

Required by the Google AdMob advertising SDK to load and display banner ads. No image data is sent over this connection.

ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE

Required by AdMob to check network availability before requesting an ad. No user data is transmitted.

The app does not request camera access, microphone access, contacts, location, or background access of any kind.

On-Device Machine Learning

All AI features are powered by Google ML Kit running locally on your device:

  • Face detection — processes image pixels locally, no biometric data is stored
  • Text recognition (OCR) — extracts text locally to find emails, phones, IBANs, card numbers
  • Barcode / QR scanning — decodes locally, no scan history is kept

ML Kit's on-device models do not send data to Google. See ML Kit Terms of Service.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

Screenshot Redactor is free and supported by banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve relevant ads:

Data type Purpose Shared with
Advertising ID (GAID) Ad personalisation & frequency capping Google LLC
Device information Ad rendering & targeting Google LLC
IP address Coarse location for ad relevance Google LLC

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or Opt out of Ads Personalisation (older Android).

AdMob's full privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Screenshot Redactor is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app has no social features, accounts, or user-generated content.

Data Retention

We retain no data. Images processed by the app exist only in your device's memory during the session and are cleared when you leave the app. Saved redacted images are written to your device's local gallery (Pictures/Redacted/) and are under your full control.
